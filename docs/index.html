How it works

Every prediction below comes from a Dixon-Coles goal model — per-team attack/defence ratings fit by maximum likelihood on 49,000+ internationals (1872–present), with squad market value folded in as a prior. It outputs win/draw/loss probabilities and a most-likely scoreline. Predictions are locked before kickoff and never edited; the git history of the prediction ledger is the proof.

An honest finding: rigorously evaluated, this is a well-calibrated forecaster but not a market-beating one — its disagreements with bookmaker odds track its own small calibration drift, not genuine edge. The project documents what was tried and what didn't work (rest-days, naïve market value, temperature recalibration) as much as what did.
